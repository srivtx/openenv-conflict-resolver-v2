Training Analytics (SFT + GRPO)
Numbers below come from a Colab T4 run. The holdout pool uses seeds 9000-9099 (disjoint from training seeds 1000-1999); the adversarial pool uses seeds 5000-5009 and probes that the grader's anti-shortcut behavior holds.
Pool Untrained 3B After SFT After SFT + GRPO Status
Holdout (n=40) 0.5454 0.9877 0.9876 +0.4423 lift
Adversarial (n=10) — — 0.9885 Shortcuts dead
Takeaway: the +0.4423 absolute lift on holdout from untrained → SFT shows the model is learning intent routing on procedurally-novel episodes. GRPO ≈ SFT here (0.9876 vs 0.9877) — on this task the SFT stage is doing most of the lifting and GRPO is keeping the score stable.

Architecture highlights
  • World state across steps — calendar mutations, pending clarifications, and cascade queue all carry forward; actual state, not bookkeeping.
  • Two-step partial observability — ask_clarification keeps the same conflict at the front of the queue and re-presents it next step with the revealed info.
  • Real cascades — rescheduling past 18:00 with owner=work spawns a follow-on school-pickup conflict on the queue. Tested under perfect-play oracle.
  • Rigorous grader — regex-strict HH:MM slot scoring, length+verb+diversity message scoring, deterministic and probe-tested.
  • Disjoint train/holdout/adversarial seed pools — asserted at module import.
